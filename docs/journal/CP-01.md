#### Partnership email draft

```
Subject: Partnership inquiry — BirdieIQ analytics (read-only user data)

Hello 18Birdies team,

I'm building BirdieIQ, a golf analytics product that helps players analyze
round history (strengths, weaknesses, trends, and practice plans) from their
own data.

Many of our early users keep their rounds in 18Birdies. We are not looking to
scrape or circumvent your app — we're interested in an official, user-consented
way to access round and stats data (API, partner program, or data portability
workflow) similar to how other golf platforms integrate with partners.

Would your business or product team be open to a short call to discuss:
- Whether a read-only API or partner integration exists today
- GDPR/CCPA export formats and whether structured round data is included
- Guidelines for analytics apps in your ecosystem

Happy to share a one-pager and demo timeline.
```
